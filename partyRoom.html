How to plan a party according to eventcreate.com

  1. Build a Budget
    • Venue
    • Entertainment
    • Decorations
    • Food
    • Invitations
  2. Choose a Party Theme
    • Masquerade Ball
    • Roaring 20s
    • Beach Party
    • Superheroes
    • Fiesta
  3. Set a Date and Time
    • How long do you want your party to be? If your party is multiple hours, how will you keep your guests entertained?
    • Will your event be during dinner time? If so, do you need to feed your guests a full meal?
    • When will your event end? If it’s a school or work night, consider ending the party on the earlier side. If it’s the weekend, keep the party going!
    • If your event is on the weekend, is it a holiday weekend? If so, will your guests already have plans?
    • If your event is on a weekday, when do your guests get out of school or work? Will they need extra time to go home before going to your party?
